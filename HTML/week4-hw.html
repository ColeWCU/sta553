Looking at the chart the relationship seems to be that penguins with larger bills also are likely to have larger flippers. However, the species has an effect as well. The Gentoo penguins tend to be larger than the Adelie penguins in both bill and flipper size. The trend is less present in the Gentoo penguins, they all have fairly similar flipper size but differ in bill length. It would appear this is a case of Simpsons paradox and the species is most important.
